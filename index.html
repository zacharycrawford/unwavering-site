Un(wavering)

Poetry by Zachary Crawford

"Un(wavering)" explores the delicate tension between certainty and doubt through vivid imagery and raw emotion. This collection invites readers to embrace vulnerability and find strength in imperfection.

Excerpt:

Will I find the end to this stream,
or simply another beginning?
Is an artist still an artist,
if the world destroys his gleam?

Excerpt:

Whatever happened to depth of life?
Meaning; less the strife.
